05Letting Go System

The feature no other app has built.

Market Differentiator

Every competitor assumes your goal is fixed and their only job is to track it. DreamCoach is the first app to acknowledge that sometimes, letting go is growth. This is a four-step structured ceremony — not a delete button.

1

Reflection

A guided prompt to articulate why this dream no longer serves the person you're becoming. Private. Unhurried. No judgment.

2

Recognition

Acknowledging what the journey taught you. What you gained. What you learned about yourself. The dream wasn't wasted — it built you.

3

Release Ceremony

A symbolic, meaningful in-app ritual for closure. Written or interactive. The moment of intentional release — not abandonment.

4

What's Next

The option to return to active dreaming with new focus, or to hold the space open. Letting go creates room — DreamCoach honours that.

"Letting go isn't giving up.
It's making room for what's next."
This framing is genuinely press-worthy. No wellness app has ever said this. It's the hook for a full PR campaign at launch.
